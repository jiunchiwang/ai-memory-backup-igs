**Learned**: Global ~/.claude/settings.json with "model": "opus[1m]" overrides bridge's acp-providers.json "claude-fable-5" configuration. Claude Code's global settings layer takes precedence over ACP's set_config_option, causing model pin to fail or be overwritten.

**#S938** Clarification on Feature Wheel respin mechanics: whether reels 1 and 6 must both be locked during respin (Jun 23, 10:38 AM)

**Investigated**: Specification documents reviewed for respin trigger conditions across multiple Feature Wheel functions (symbol transformation, multiplier, score upgrade). References checked: [C234], [C245], [C257], [B124], [B27], [B31]

**Learned**: Respin trigger condition requires "at least 1 COLLECT symbol" on the board (single reel sufficient), not both reels 1 and 6. The "locking reels 1 and 6" described in [B124] is the respin mechanism (how respin operates), not the trigger condition (when respin activates). COLLECT symbols only appear on reels 1 and 6 per [B27], so the practical requirement is reel 1 OR reel 6 has a COLLECT symbol. Having 2 COLLECT symbols triggers two separate collection actions per [B31], but is not required for respin to occur.

**Completed**: Specification clarification delivered with documented evidence from game design specs, distinguishing trigger conditions from execution mechanics

**#S984** Investigate whether Active_Red's Lightframe grid layer appears on symbolPrefab and document findings in markdown (Jun 25, 7:23 PM)

**Investigated**: Analyzed the architecture of Target_Explode.skel animation system, examining how Active_Red's Lightframe layer relates to symbolPrefab, including asset layer symbol skeleton slots, runtime skeleton/pool/parent layers, and prefab nesting structure

**Learned**: Active_Red's grid (Lightframe) layer cannot appear on symbolPrefab due to architectural constraints: asset layer symbol skeleton has no corresponding slots (x0), runtime uses independent skeleton/pool/parent layers, and prefab layer has no nesting. The full-board 5×5 grid visual is actually baked into Target_Explode.skel's Active_Red animation itself, combining single-cell explosion with full-board LightFrame in one skeleton. Multiple targets calling `PlayTargetExplodeEffect` with `setWorldPosition(worldPos)` may cause overlapping effects when positioned to single-cell coordinates

**Completed**: Root cause analysis completed identifying that the full-board grid is part of the explosion animation design itself, not a layering bug. Identified potential misalignment/ghosting issue requiring art/design team review
